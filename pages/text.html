The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been starting out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk. lying between the copies Nature. New Scientist. and all the other scientific journals of her work had appeared in. was well thumbed copy of On the road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college. and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

she had spent the last ten years in this rrom sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of the people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work. unraveling the secrets of the 11ama DNA. to be an outstanding achievement. Molly did think she had something better to do

Being the richest man in the cemetery doesn't matter to me. Going to bed at night saying we've done something wonderful, that's what matters to me.
